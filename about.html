WHO ARE WE?

Founded in 2010 by PETE IRELAND, Opening Act Films is a boutique screen production outfit specialising in indie film, episodic, and corporate creative content. Opening Act Films has created award winning films that have been screened in film festivals and on television, both in Australia and internationally.

We also have a diverse slate of TV and Film Projects in development, several short films in distribution phase, and have provided media solutions for a number of corporate clients.

We tell visual stories here.

Stories for the big screen and small. Stories to entertain, provoke and inspire people. And, stories that help companies communicate with their audiences. You can watch our work in the VIDEO GALLERY.

You can read about how we've helped businesses tell their stories in CREATIVE SERVICES.

You can also subscribe to our famous weekly newsletter 'Tales From the Opening Act.....' HERE, and keep up to date with our work on the LATEST NEWS page.

MISSION

WHAT'S OUR MISSION?

To develop, produce and deliver original high quality film, television and media. This should always start with the most important element: the desire to tell great stories.

SERVICES

WHAT'S OUR SERVICES?

First and foremost we are content creators! Opening Act Films develops and produces in-house original screen stories.

Opening Act Films also offers script and story consultation services, but only to people we really like.

For businesses in need of CREATIVE SERVICES, Opening Act Films also provides development, production and delivery of high quality media solutions.

So, if you are interested in speaking with Opening Act Films about a project, politics, climate change or the current state of the corduroy trouser market, head to our CONTACT page.
