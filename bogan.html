BOGAN MOTHS

2023 | 6 x 14min Eps | Coming soon

Role: Writer / Director

Coming soon in 2023, Romp is the spin-off from Love Bytes, the viral hit web-series that has over 9 million views and 14 thousand subscribers. Housemates, Jade, Stacey and Michael are the Neapolitan ice-cream of sexuality. Young, sexy and single they search for love and lust but instead find humiliation and misadventure in Sydney’s herteroflexible dating scene.
