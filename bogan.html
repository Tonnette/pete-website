BOGAN MOTHS

8 X 22 mins | English

Tony Radevski & Pete Ireland

Produced by: Opening Acts Films

Series in development



An adult animated comedy series set amongst families of Bogong Moths, who live amongst a low-income community of humans.

Surrounded by their own form of nocturnal delights - their insatiable desire for light - the moths struggle with addiction to bulbs and blue zapper lights, where overindulgence can kill you.

But now some of them are struggling with the existential question of life and their place within it. They are starting to realise that living their days hidden and their nights wasted, isn't all it's cracked up to be.
