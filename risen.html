Risen

8 X 22 mins | English

Tony Radevski & Pete Ireland

Produced by Opening Acts Films

Series in development

SYNOPSIS

Sean roams the streets with other teenagers searching for bodies of ‘the fallen’ - reckless users of a street drug which causes people to float and then plunge to their deaths. But unlike his friends, and what seems like the rest of the world, he has a conscience. The series will be told through intersecting narratives, with Sean as the fulcrum, and observe the wider impacts of drug use in a society that is geographically and socially divided.
